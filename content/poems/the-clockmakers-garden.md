title: The Clockmaker's Garden
date: 2024-08-14
tags:

He planted gears where roses grew,
wound springs beneath the loam,
and wondered why no blossom came
to call the garden home.

The minutes ticked in ordered rows,
the hours clicked in place —
yet nothing living wished to bloom
in that mechanical space.

At last he set his tools aside,
and wept upon the ground,
and in the salt of one good tear,
a violet was found.
